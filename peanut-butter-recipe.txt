Note: Recipe have not been tested. Experiment it with your own risk!

Ingredients:
 - 1 tablespoon peanut butter
 - 1 teaspoon butter or margarine (butter recommended)
 - (optional) 1/2 teaspoon sugar 

Directions:
 1. In a coffee cup or bowl, put peanut butter and butter (or margarine). Add
 sugar if wanted.
 2. Microware it for bout 15 seconds
 3. Stir
 4. Microware more if necessary. 
 5. Enjoy! Try it with pieces of an apple.
